Texas Toast Grilled Cheese

Texas toast with a chunk of cheese in the middle, what's not to love?

Grilled cheese is one of the most recognizable american lunches. Since it's such a great staple, that begs the question. How can we take this even further?

In my opinion, we build up. Grab a loaf of texas toast, bags full of your favorite cheese, a little mayo, some parmesan, and let's stack up something monsterous!

Ingredients:

  1. Two slices of texas toast
  2. Four slices of munster cheese
  3. One handful of mozzerella cheese
  4. Four slices of pepperjack cheese
  5. One handful of cheddar cheese
  6. Two tablespoons of mayonase
  7. Two teaspoons of parmesan
