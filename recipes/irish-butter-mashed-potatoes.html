Irish Butter Mashed Potatoes

The secret butter that's worth it's weight in gold

Have you ever been out shopping at the grocery store and noticed a overly expensive type of butter that chooses to identify as irish? Well, the price increases for a reason, and the best way to make use of this golden block of dairy is to throw it in a pot of mashedpotatoes!

Irish butter adds a sort of richness to the butter flavor, sort of like sharp cheddar to mild. Ofcourse, mashed potatoes are able to make full use of absorbing and dispersing all that great flavor!

Ingredients:

  1. Potatoes
  2. Irish butter
  3. Cream cheese
  4. Milk
